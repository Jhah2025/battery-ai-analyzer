Battery Qualification Requirement Sheet

1. Voltage Safety Requirement
During discharge testing, the cell terminal voltage shall not fall below 3.00 V at any point in the measured profile.

2. Power Characterization Requirement
For qualification review, any operating period with terminal power magnitude above 5.0 W shall be flagged as high-power behavior and reviewed for expected algorithm response.

3. Documentation Requirement
Each qualification run shall include a short engineering summary covering voltage range, current behavior, power behavior, and any notable model-fitting observations.

4. Model Review Requirement
Equivalent circuit model fitting may be used for first-pass interpretation. If a simplified OCV assumption is used, the result shall be labeled as preliminary and subject to further validation with SOC-dependent OCV calibration.

5. Data Review Requirement
Requirement checks should combine measured battery metrics with relevant engineering context.
